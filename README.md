OpenFOS — Open Founder Operating System
The operating system Silicon Valley never open-sourced. Until now.
What it is

OpenFOS is a free, open-source system of 24 step-by-step skills that walks any founder anywhere in the world from raw idea to fundable, scalable business
Each skill is a structured, AI-powered tool built on the same frameworks that elite founders in Silicon Valley use: Bill Aulet's disciplined entrepreneurship, Geoffrey Moore's beachhead strategy, Clayton Christensen's Jobs-to-Be-Done, the Sequoia pitch logic, the YC discovery playbook
The full journey is organized into FOUNDRY — Foundation, Outline, User, Nurture, Discipline, Results, Yield; a deliberate sequence that mirrors how the world's best startups actually get built
OpenFOS is powered by Claude (Anthropic's AI), which means you are not reading a static PDF or sitting through a webinar you are working with an intelligent system that adapts to your specific business, your market, and your stage


The problem it solves

A first-time founder in San Antonio, Lagos, Bangalore, or São Paulo faces the exact same brutal decisions as one in San Francisco but without a YC partner to call, without a Stanford alumni network to tap, and without the unspoken playbooks passed quietly from mentor to protégé in the most privileged ecosystems
That inequality is not about intelligence or ambition. It is about access to infrastructure. OpenFOS is that infrastructure free, open, and available now


The bold truth about accelerators

Traditional startup accelerators and incubators charge 6% to 10% equity for what is essentially access to mentors, a cohort of peers, and a demo day stage
That is not a small number. On a $10M exit, that is $600K to $1M of your life's work for meetings, workshops, and introductions you could have navigated yourself
On a $100M exit, you just handed someone $6M to $10M for a 12-week program
OpenFOS gives you the structured frameworks, the decision tools, and the AI-powered execution support without touching your cap table
Your equity is your ownership. It is the result of your risk, your sacrifice, and your vision. Protect it.


What AI changes for founders

For the first time in history, a founder with no network, no MBA, and no accelerator backing can access institutional-grade thinking on demand not through expensive consultants or gatekept communities, but through AI
OpenFOS leverages Claude to compress what used to take months of mentorship and expensive workshops into focused, structured, interactive working sessions at any hour, in any timezone, in any language
You are not just reading frameworks. You are running them. The AI asks you the hard questions, stress-tests your assumptions, surfaces the blind spots, and produces investor-ready outputs in real time
This is not a chatbot giving you generic startup advice. This is the startup scaling value chain from problem validation to exit planning running in your browser, for free


Who this is for

The engineer in nantucket, Nashik, Nairobi or who has a real insight but has never written a pitch deck

The bottom line

You do not need a $50,000 accelerator program or a Palo Alto zip code to build a world-class company. You need the right frameworks, the right questions, and the discipline to execute. OpenFOS gives you all three — powered by AI, for free, forever.
